Meet our owner, Esther.

Esther is a familiar face around London.

Zimbabwe-born, Esther has worked in a number of management roles at many luxury hotels and restaurants in the U.S. - most recently, as the Resident Manager for The Clifton Inn in London. Before that, she worked with Boar’s Head Inn, a four-diamond property that’s also located in London, where she oversaw the main restaurant, bar, and room service. Internationally, Esther also has a vast array of experience with major establishments overseas.

Esther’s strengths include a cheerful, hardworking attitude, and years of experience working in luxury hotels and restaurants. She has a keen attention to detail, and brings top quality service and knowledge to Santa Barbra Cleaning Services.
